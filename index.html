Hello, I'm Tram

Welcome to my portfolio! I am an enthusiastic Full Stack Web Developer who is passionate about creating impactful web applications. Though I’m just starting my career, I’ve dedicated myself to learning and mastering both front-end and back-end technologies.

Who I am

I am a motivated and proactive Frontend Developer with a degree in Computing Studies and Information System from Douglas College and a year dedicated to enhancing my technical skills and industry knowledge. Although my gap year did not involve formal employment, I spent this time intensively building my expertise in frontend technologies and honing my development skills.

During this period, I focused on learning and applying HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React through personal projects, online courses, and coding challenges. I actively built a portfolio of web applications, demonstrating my ability to create responsive and user-friendly designs. My commitment to continuous learning and skill development has kept me up-to-date with the latest industry trends and best practices.

From my study years to the present moment, I also spent my time working as a Food and Beverage Server. This part time job has equipped me with valuable skills such as attention to detail, effective communication, and the ability to thrive in fast-paced environments. These skills are directly applicable to frontend development, where precision, clear communication, and problem-solving are crucial in my opinion.

I am enthusiastic about applying my self-taught skills and knowledge to contribute to a dynamic development team. I am eager to bring my technical capabilities and problem-solving mindset to a frontend developer role and help build engaging and impactful web applications.

In my free time, I love to practice yoga, playing with my cats and cleaning the house, or simply hangout with friends over a ☕ coffee.
